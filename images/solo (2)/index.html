About the studio

A small team with a wide range and a fixed standard.

We started Solo to prove that a lean group of specialists can out-ship a big agency. No account layers, no hand-offs that lose the plot — the people who scope your project are the people who make it. That keeps the work sharp and the timeline honest.

  • One team, end to end. Strategy, design and code sit in the same room, so nothing gets lost in translation.
  • Fixed scope, fixed price. You get a clear quote and a firm date before we start — no creeping invoices.
  • Built to hand over. Clean files, documented components and a walkthrough so your team can run it solo.
  • Ship in weeks, not quarters. Tight sprints and weekly demos keep momentum from kickoff to launch.

What we do

Six ways we make things move.

Pick one or hand us the whole journey. Every engagement is scoped to your stage — a first brand, a rebuild, or the growth work after launch.

Brand Strategy

Positioning, naming and messaging that give the rest of the work a spine. We find the one true thing your audience should remember.

Web Design

Responsive marketing sites and landing pages with a clear story, honest copy and a layout that reads premium on any screen.

Development

Fast, accessible front-ends built on clean, framework-friendly code. Bootstrap 5, no jQuery, and a build your team can maintain.

Product & UX

App and dashboard design grounded in real flows. We prototype, test and refine so the interface earns its keep.

Motion & Video

Product explainers, launch films and micro-animations that make an interface feel alive without slowing it down.

Growth & SEO

The work after launch: technical SEO, content structure and analytics wired in so the site keeps compounding.
