Solo — Free One-Page Multipurpose Website Template

Solo is a free, polished one-page multipurpose website template for agencies, startups, freelancers and small businesses, designed in-house by uiCookies. It's built on Bootstrap 5 with a heavy custom design layer (a warm paper neutral base, near-black ink type and a single confident cobalt accent, all driven by CSS custom properties) and packs a sticky scrollspy navbar, hero, about, services, a filterable portfolio with lightbox, a count-up metrics band, team, three-tier pricing with a monthly/annual toggle, a testimonials slider, a journal teaser and a validated contact form onto one calm scroll. It is completely jQuery-free — every interaction is plain vanilla JavaScript with progressive enhancement, keyboard support and reduced-motion awareness — and it's free for both personal and commercial use under the uiCookies license (attribution required). Replace the demo images and copy with your own before going live.
